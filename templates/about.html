    COLLEGE HISTORY

  • Swami inaugurated the Sri Satya Sai Arts and Science, college for women at Anantapur and 22nd July 1968, in the Campus of the then govt girls high school. The public meeting held in this connection, was in the presence of Sri T.V Raghavulu A.P Ministor for education and Dr Suri Bhagavantham an illustrious Scientist in this country who became the director of IISC Bengaluru in the later years.
  • During his address Swamy 0bserved “I have allowed this college to raise because it will instill in the minds of the students the ancient ideals of Satya, Dharma ,Shanthi and Prema, ideals that are delianated in the vedas, described in shastras, illustrated in epics, practised by countless generations of the men and women of this land and confirmed as best suited for individual and social progress by its saints and sages, law-givers and leaders, for centuries”.
  • Swamy constructed a beautiful Iconic building in the outskirts of the Anantapur Town, On 8th July 1971 this college was shifted to that building. The Inauguration function was attended by the then President of India Sri V.V. Giri and the then Chief Minister of A.P Sri K. Brahmananda Reddy.
  • Four blocks of this premises i.e, College building with beautiful elevation, auditorium, library and hostel blocks were constructed in a record time of 10 months Dr. Suri Bhagavantham expressed “The college at Anantapur is concrete example of something super human…..If you want to see divinity in action, you can find concrete evidence at Anantapur”.
  • In his inaugural address Swamy observed “The seed has been planted, it will sprout and spread, heavy with fruits.”
  • At present the extent of this sprawling premises is 114 acres.
  • This college was running Pre Universities courses i.e. the present Intermediate courses, Under graduation courses and Post Graduation courses. It was a Residential College with a hostel facility.
  • Sri Satya Sai Arts and Science Junior College
  • Swamy constructed a beautiful Iconic building in the outskirts of the Anantapur Town, On 8th July 1971 this college was shifted to that building. The Inauguration function was attended by the then President of India Sri V.V. Giri and the then Chief Minister of A.P Sri K. Brahmananda Reddy.
  • SSS Arts and Science Junior college for women
  • Later on, for administrative reasons the Intermediate course was sought to be bifurcated. Swamy constructed this beautiful building in the Heart of the Anantapur Town, so that the local girl students could easily commute to the college. It started functioning from 1-6-1995. This magnificent building with beautiful sculpture of goddess Saraswathi has been the alma mater for thousands of students since then.
  • Since 1995 till September 2021, this college functioned as aided college, that means govt was paying salaries for some posts. With effect from 1-10 -2021, the aided staff was taken over by govt and since then it is functioning as private college.
  • Its area is about 4 acres. The premises is owned by Sri Satya Sai Central Trust Puttaparthi.

    FEE DETAILS

  • The college fees has been very minimum. At present while the A.P Govt fixed maximum Rupees of 20,000, we are collecting only 1/3rd of this maximum limit that is around Rs. 7,000. This fees structure has been attracting students from marginalised sections of society that too from surrounding rural areas. About 70% of our students hail from socially and economically backward classes and minorities.
